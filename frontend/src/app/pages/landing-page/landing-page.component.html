About this platform

I'm Panagiotis Tsagkouris, and together with my mentor, Andreas Menychtas, I've brought to life an innovative platform as part of my thesis project. The goal is to reshape the future of healthcare. Imagine a world where contacting your doctor and scheduling appointments is not just faster but also more efficient than ever before. With this platform, it's a reality. From managing appointment schedules to offering medical advice and accessing patient histories, everything happens seamlessly and instantly, without the need for direct human interaction.

Who is this platform for

Doctors

Doctors who need assistant contacting with patients, managing their schedule and collecting info about their patients

Patients

Patients who need to contact a doctor without needing to wait in telephone line or email response from someone.
